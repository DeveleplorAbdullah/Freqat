WHO WE ARE? >>>

Established in 2004, Fregat LLC, today provides complete spectrum of marine services, including ship agency services, ship lay-up, vessel equipment & spare parts and port services etc. We have excellent business relationships with our clients from reputed Oil and Gas industries and have experienced considerable growth, over the last couple of years, mainly through acquisitions. Through our experience and knowledge of industry we maintain Procurement, Logistics, Custom clearance, Ship repair, Maintenance, Agency and other required services in marine industry.

Cooperation with world's leading suppliers in the industry, as well as knowledge of local legislation and business procedures gives us considerable competitive advantage in the Caspian region.

We aim at standardizing processes and aligning product ranges and services at the highest possible quality, in order to live up to your expectations when you do business with Fregat. We are capable to serve our customers through our extended network, thus ensures precise delivery of all the supplies, equipment and services you need in the whole region.

The dexterous team of professionals, who hold in-depth knowledge in this domain is available 24/7 to maintain the fastest and efficient services. Every task carried out, every decision taken, is deeply embedded in the values and business conduct of our company: high ethics, human touch, innovation and dynamics.

Our commercial success is based on the ability to work hand-in-hand with our customers and achieving their confidence and trust. Finally we are committed to deliver our services in the most secure and environmental friendly way.
